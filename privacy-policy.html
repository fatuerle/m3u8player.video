Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how M3U8Player.video ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website. We are committed to handling your data transparently and in accordance with applicable privacy laws, including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Data Controller

The data controller responsible for this website is M3U8Player.video. For any privacy-related enquiries, you may contact us at: support@m3u8player.video

2. Data We Collect and How We Use It

2.1 Local Storage (Your Device Only)

We do not collect, store, or transmit the video stream URLs you play. All playback runs entirely within your browser.

  • Playback history: Recent stream URLs are saved in your browser's localStorage solely to improve your experience. This data never leaves your device and is never sent to our servers. You can delete it at any time using the "Clear history" button on the player page, or via your browser settings.
  • Theme preference: Your chosen light/dark theme setting is stored locally in localStorage.
  • Cookie consent: Your Accept or Decline choice is stored in localStorage so the banner does not reappear on subsequent visits.

2.2 Analytics and Advertising Cookies

If you Accept cookies via our consent banner, we load Google AdSense on this site. Google, as a third-party vendor, may use cookies and similar technologies to serve personalised advertisements based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

If you Decline cookies, no advertising or analytics scripts are loaded. You will still be able to use all core functionality of the player.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process personal data only where we have a valid legal basis under GDPR Article 6:

  • Consent (Article 6(1)(a)): Loading Google AdSense and associated advertising cookies only occurs after you have given explicit consent via the cookie banner. You may withdraw consent at any time by clearing your browser's local storage and cookies.
  • Legitimate Interests (Article 6(1)(f)): Basic, non-identifiable server log data (e.g. anonymous IP addresses, HTTP request logs retained for security and abuse prevention) may be processed by our hosting provider. We ensure this does not override your fundamental rights.

4. Google AdSense and Third-Party Advertising

When consent is granted, Google AdSense is used to display advertisements. Google and its partners may:

  • Use cookies (including the DoubleClick DART cookie) to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites.
  • Collect data about your browsing behaviour for ad personalisation and measurement.

You can opt out of personalised advertising at any time by visiting Google Ad Settings, or opt out of third-party advertising networks via aboutads.info.

For full details on how Google handles data, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

5. International Data Transfers

Google's servers are located in the United States and other countries outside the European Economic Area (EEA). When advertising cookies are loaded (with your consent), your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States. Google relies on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission as the transfer mechanism. For more information, see Google's GDPR commitments.

6. Data Retention

  • Local storage data (playback history, theme, consent flag): Retained on your device until you clear it manually or clear your browser data. No server-side copy is kept.
  • Advertising and analytics data collected by Google: Governed by Google's own retention policies, as described in their Privacy Policy.

7. Your Rights (EEA, UK, and Switzerland)

If you are located in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, you have the following rights under GDPR (or equivalent local law):

  • Right of Access: You may request confirmation of whether we hold personal data about you and a copy of that data.
  • Right to Rectification: You may request correction of inaccurate personal data we hold.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): You may request deletion of your personal data where there is no compelling reason for us to continue processing it.
  • Right to Restriction of Processing: You may request that we restrict how we use your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to Data Portability: You may request a copy of data you have provided to us in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: You may object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing purposes.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time. This does not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@m3u8player.video. We will respond within 30 days.

Because we store data only in your own browser's local storage, exercising most of these rights can be done immediately and directly by clearing your browser data — no request to us is necessary for locally stored data.

8. Right to Lodge a Complaint

If you believe your data protection rights have not been respected, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority. A list of EU data protection authorities is available at edpb.europa.eu. UK users may contact the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).

9. Third-Party Privacy Policies

This Privacy Policy applies only to M3U8Player.video. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services such as Google. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

10. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

11. Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in law or our practices. The latest version is always available on this page. Where changes are material, we will update the date shown above.

For any questions about this policy, please contact us via our Contact Page or at support@m3u8player.video.
