About M3U8Player.video

Last Updated: May 27, 2026

M3U8Player.video is a browser-based tool for testing and playing user-provided HLS and M3U8 streams quickly, without installing desktop software.

What This Tool Does

  • Plays valid M3U8 and HLS links directly in your browser.
  • Helps test stream availability and playback behavior.
  • Keeps recent stream history in your local browser storage.

What This Tool Does Not Do

  • Does not host or distribute any video content.
  • Does not proxy user-provided stream links.
  • Does not provide IPTV channel lists or copyrighted media.

Compliance and Responsibility

Users are responsible for ensuring they have legal rights to access any stream URL they test. If you are a copyright owner, please contact the hosting provider of the source stream.

Contact

For support, feedback, or policy questions, use our Contact page or email support@m3u8player.video.
